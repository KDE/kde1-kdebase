8. International Keyboard - Startup

The Startup screen configures how the International Keyboard is started, and the placement of its keyboard-indicator button.

  • Autostart: If this box is checked, the International Keyboard will be started automatically at the beginning of each KDE session.
  • Docked: If this box is checked, the keyboard-indicator button will be docked into the panel.
  • Place: If the ``Docked'' box is not checked, the keyboard-indicator button will be placed at one of the four corners of the display. The pull-down Place menu presents a list of choices for this location.
